Generic Cookie Base
---
Description: Toll House Cookie Base
Yield: 5 dozen cookies
---
Base Ingredients
270 g all-purpose flour
6 g baking soda
6 g salt
227 g butter
150 g granulated sugar
165 g packed brown sugar
88 g eggs
Flavor Ingredients
5 g vanilla extract
---
Instructions
Step 1: Preheat oven to 375Â° F.
Step 2: Combine flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
Step 3: Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
